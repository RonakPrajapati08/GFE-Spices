Spices Generally speaking, a spice is any seed, fruit, root, bark, or other plant material that is used to flavor or color food. Herbs, which are plant leaves, blossoms, or stems used as flavoring or garnish, are distinguished from spices.

Pulses

A plant belonging to the Fabaceae family or its fruit or seed is referred to as a legume. The seed is also known as a pulse when utilized as a dry grain. Agriculture is used to grow legumes, mostly for human use.
